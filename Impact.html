Our Impact

Since our inception, HelpBridgeKenya has touched thousands of vulnerable lives across Kenya, providing hope, healing, and tangible support. Here are some of the highlights of what we have achieved together with our partners and supporters:

5,000+ Lives Connected

Single mothers, widows, and street children linked with NGOs offering financial aid, education, shelter, and counseling.

Education Scholarships

Over 1,200 children sponsored with full scholarships and school supplies through our education partners.

Safe Shelter Provided

Hundreds of street children and abused women have found safe, temporary housing thanks to our shelter programs.

Mental Health Support

Thousands have received counseling and therapy to overcome trauma and build emotional resilience.

Community Outreach

Regular awareness campaigns on health, human rights, and empowerment conducted in multiple Kenyan counties.
