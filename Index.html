Empowering Vulnerable Lives

HelpBridgeKenya connects single mothers, widows, and street children with NGOs and organizations providing support, funding, education, and empowerment.

About Us

HelpBridgeKenya is a humanitarian initiative dedicated to linking vulnerable individuals—single mothers, widows, and street children—with NGOs and organizations providing financial, emotional, educational, and social support. Our mission is to create a bridge of hope, opportunity, and transformation.

Our Programs

  • Single Mothers Uplift: Vocational training, childcare, and micro-financing opportunities.
  • Street Children Rescue: Rehabilitation centers and education sponsorship programs.
  • Widows Empowerment: Grief counseling, income generation training, and housing solutions.

Our Impact

Over 5,000 lives connected with aid organizations. Education scholarships, food programs, safe shelters, and emotional support made possible through HelpBridgeKenya.

Our Partners

We collaborate with reputable NGOs and foundations worldwide.
