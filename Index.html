Connecting Vulnerable Lives with Hope

HelpBridgeKenya links single mothers, widows, and street children with NGOs and organizations that provide support, funding, education, and empowerment.

About Us

HelpBridgeKenya is a humanitarian initiative dedicated to empowering vulnerable groups by linking them with NGOs, donors, and support systems. We aim to create a bridge of hope, resources, and opportunity.

What We Do

  • Counseling & Therapy Services
  • Emergency Helplines & Mental Health Support
  • Community Outreach & Awareness
  • Volunteer Training & Empowerment

Our Impact

Over 5,000 lives connected with aid organizations. Education scholarships, food programs, safe shelters, and emotional support made possible through HelpBridgeKenya.
