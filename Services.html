Our Support Programs & Services

At HelpBridgeKenya, we offer a range of programs designed to uplift and empower vulnerable individuals, including single mothers, widows, and street children. Here’s how we help:

  • Counseling & Therapy Services: Professional emotional and psychological support to help individuals cope with trauma, grief, and stress.
  • Emergency Helplines & Mental Health Support: 24/7 helplines connecting people in crisis to trained counselors.
  • Community Outreach & Awareness: Educational programs that inform communities about health, rights, and social services.
  • Volunteer Training & Empowerment: Preparing volunteers with skills to effectively support and mentor beneficiaries.
  • Financial Aid & Micro-financing: Grants and micro-loans to help single mothers and widows start small businesses.
  • Education Sponsorships: Scholarships and school supplies for street children and vulnerable youth.
  • Safe Shelter & Rehabilitation: Temporary housing and rehabilitation for street children and women escaping abuse.
